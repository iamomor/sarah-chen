**Category:** Neighborhood Guides  
**Title:** Buyer's Guide to Austin's Premier Neighborhoods  
**Short Description:** An overview of Tarrytown, Westlake, Hyde Park, and other premium central Austin areas.

Choosing the right Austin neighborhood involves balancing lifestyle, commute times, architectural preferences, and school districts. Here is a breakdown of our primary markets:

* **Vibe:** Historic, tree-lined, prestigious.
* **Architecture:** 1920s Tudors, classic colonial estates, and modern rebuilds.

### 2. Westlake (Eanes ISD)
* **Vibe:** Hill country luxury, scenic views, family-oriented.

### 3. Hyde Park (Central/North)
* **Vibe:** Historic charm, pedestrian-friendly, bohemian luxury.
* **Architecture:** Meticulously preserved Victorian homes and classic craftsman bungalows.
* **Highlight:** A vibrant local dining scene, historic parks, and proximity to the University of Texas.

* **Vibe:** Modern, creative, active outdoor lifestyle.
* **Architecture:** Mid-century ranches, ultra-modern custom architecture.
* **Highlight:** Easy access to the Barton Creek Greenbelt, Lady Bird Lake, and the South Congress (SoCo) shopping corridor.

## Article 4: Navigating the Luxury Jumbo Mortgage & Carry Costs

**Category:** Financial Planning  
**Title:** Navigating the Luxury Jumbo Mortgage & Carry Costs  
**Short Description:** Critical details on jumbo loan structures, interest rates, and down payments in Central Texas.

### Article Body:

When purchasing a high-end property in Austin, standard conforming loan limits are typically exceeded, requiring a Jumbo Mortgage. Here is what you need to know:

### Conforming vs. Jumbo Loans
Conforming loans adhere to limits set by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac. Any loan amount above the current conforming threshold is classified as a Jumbo Loan, which carries distinct qualification standards.

### Key Requirements for Jumbo Loans:
* **Higher Down Payment:** Expect to put down a minimum of 20% to 30% for luxury residential purchases.
* **Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio:** Lenders typically require a DTI ratio below 38% to 43%.
* **Reserve Assets:** Lenders often request proof of 6 to 12 months of mortgage payments (principal, interest, taxes, insurance) held in cash or liquid retirement assets.
* **Double Appraisals:** For properties priced over $1.5M or $2M, lenders commonly require two independent home appraisals to verify value.
