### History and Character

Hyde Park’s character is defined by its diversity of form. The neighborhood is a visual feast of meticulously restored Queen Anne Victorians, cozy Craftsman bungalows, and charming Tudor Revivals. Walking through the tree-lined streets, you’ll notice the signature local style—front porches adorned with rocking chairs, drought-tolerant gardens, and a commitment to preserving the historic scale of the neighborhood. This is a place where history isn't just in the books; it’s in the stained glass windows and the original shiplap walls of the homes that line the historic avenues.
